O1. ----------------- WHAT WE DO.?

Who We Are and What We Do?

We are Simba Developers,Provide Technical knowledge and backbone to the project. We guide you and turn your ideas into proper planning,we convert your imagination into reality with you.We will provide you with professional team for planning out the idea, create documents,follow procedure and execute the project professionally and successfully.





Planning

Once we get in touch with your idea , and is approved by our CTO, we will put together a team of experts for the job, let you know each and every step.We will invite you to our firm, plan the whole project ,steps,expenditure,time required and others.

Documentation

After each step of planning, we will note them down, so we wont lose the point in coming future. Our experts will note down(document) the procedure , time management,expenditure.

Designing & Procedure

Now the project contains 2 parts, Back-end and Front-End,In Back-end,we will code 1000s of codes ,100s of Solution,and alot of research work,Debugging,trial and error method,testing etc.This will look messy without Front-End(Designing).

Execution

After Designing and Procedure, we will test the project in real life, interact with people,get feedback , find real-time errors,potential of the project.After 100% of surety ,we will declare your project as completed.
